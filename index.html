    You make the rules

    TF2 has many aspects making it a great competitive experience, but not every player wants that! TFE servers allow players to use the voting system to control many additional game functions, including toggling random crits, class limits, and server-wide voice chat! In addition to the option of class limits and random crits, TFE also features fixed bullet spread on all shotguns, and reworks of many weapons! Some weapons are more powerful, some less, and some act completely different! For the new stats of all weapons, check out our stats page!

    Bots begone!

    Our servers feature advanced protection against bots and cheaters so that you can play in peace. Joining any of our servers requires completing a captcha, and we also have an active team of admins in multiple timezones to resolve issues swiftly around the clock.

     

    Content updates

    A foreign concept to many new players, and a badly missed one to veteran players, TFE will have even more content added in free updates! You can look forward to new maps, gamemodes, and weapons.
